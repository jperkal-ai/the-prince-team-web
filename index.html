About

Meet
Prince Dockery

Prince Dockery is a Licensed Associate Real Estate Broker at The Corcoran Group's SoHo office, bringing a thorough, success-driven approach to every transaction. Over 15 years in New York real estate, Prince has built an unparalleled track record — earning a reputation as the go-to broker for athletes, entertainers, and discerning buyers and sellers.

His clients have included Carmelo Anthony, Tracy Morgan, Lady Gaga, and Adam Levine. Before launching The Prince Team, Prince honed his craft at Douglas Elliman, Town Residential, and Compass.

Today, The Prince Team operates from Downtown Manhattan with reach across Brooklyn, Westchester County, the Hamptons, and Upstate New York.
